Team Goals

Our team goal this year is to improve in areas that we lacked in last year. Time management at practices was not great, so we are working on ways to be more productive during our limited time. We would also like to improve our autonomous program this year. By focusing on developing a robot quickly, we will have more time to plan and develop an autonomous program instead of rushing last minute to complete it before a competition.
